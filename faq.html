FAQ

Clear answers to common questions.

What activities are allowed in v1?

Only BSL-1 biology and basic digital fabrication (e.g., 3D printing, laser cutting with operator). No pathogens, no hazardous chemistry, no unsupervised access.

Do minors need guardian consent?

Yes. Students under 18 must provide signed guardian consent before enrolling or booking sessions.

What is the supervision ratio?

Set by each host lab. Typical v1 ratios: fab tools 1:8, wet benches 1:4, or stricter if the host requires.

Is there insurance coverage?

Sessions operate under host lab policies and/or event liability as agreed in the MOU. Only low-risk activities are permitted.

How long is the Passport valid?

12 months from issuance, subject to revocation for safety violations.
