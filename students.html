Students

Complete the safety course to earn your Lab Readiness Passport. Then book insured, supervised sessions at partner labs.

Get the Passport → Find a Session →

Why this matters

  • Get real equipment access to build your portfolio.
  • Show safety readiness to labs and mentors.
  • Pathway to internships and projects.

Eligibility

  • Ages: HS, undergrad, recent grads.
  • Guardian consent required if under 18.
  • No prior experience required.

What you’ll do

  • 3D printing, microscopy demos, electronics soldering.
  • Operator-run instrument open days.

Pricing

  • Passport: transparent fee.
  • Sessions: per-slot price.
  • Consumables: pass-through costs.
  • Discounts: school packs.

What to bring

  • PPE: follow host policy.
  • Clothing: long pants, closed shoes.
  • Photo ID and consent form.

Outcomes

  • Badge valid 12 months.
  • Attendance record for your portfolio.
