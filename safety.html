Safety & Policies

Clear scope, supervision ratios, PPE & conduct, incident reporting, data/IP, photography, and age policy.

Scope

  • Allowed: BSL-1 biology; basic digital fabrication.
  • Excluded: pathogens, hazardous chemistry, unsupervised work.

Supervision ratios

  • Fab tools: 1:8
  • Wet benches: 1:4
  • Hosts may require stricter ratios.

PPE & conduct

  • Follow posted PPE and conduct rules at all times.
  • Zero tolerance for harassment; code of conduct enforced.

Incident reporting

Report safety incidents and near misses promptly. Conduct concerns can be reported anonymously.

Submit incident report

Data & IP

Default: hosts retain instrument data; students keep notes and learning artifacts.

Photographs

Media consent is opt-in. You control your photo permissions.

Age policy

Guardian consent required below 18; chaperone presence per host rules.
